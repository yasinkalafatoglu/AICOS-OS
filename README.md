## Overview

AICOS OS is a governance-first institutional decision intelligence infrastructure designed to support audit-ready, replayable, explainable, and human-final AI-assisted decision systems.

It is designed for high-stakes institutional environments including finance, energy, logistics, governance, and geopolitical risk analysis.

AICOS OS does not replace human authority.  
It strengthens institutional decision reliability through governance, replayability, auditability, and deterministic reasoning.

## Core Principles

- Human-Final Authority
- Governance-First AI
- Audit-Ready Decision Infrastructure
- Replayable Decision Systems
- Deterministic Governance
- Explainable Reasoning
- No Autonomous Final Execution
- Evidence-Based Decision Records

## Architecture

AICOS OS includes the following core layers:

1. Decision Intelligence Layer
2. Governance Engine
3. Replay Infrastructure
4. Audit & Evidence Layer
5. Risk Intelligence Layer
6. Executive Control Room
7. Research & Evidence Repository
