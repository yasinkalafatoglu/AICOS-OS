# AICOS Security Principles

## Governance-First Security

Security is treated as a core architectural layer within AICOS OS.

---

## Human-Final Authority

Critical actions should remain under human approval and governance oversight.

---

## Auditability

Security-relevant decision flows should remain reviewable and auditable.

---

## Replayability

Replay systems are used to support operational trust and post-event analysis.

---

## Fail-Closed Logic

When uncertainty or system integrity risks exceed acceptable thresholds, systems should halt rather than execute irreversible actions.

---

## Operational Trust

AICOS focuses on operational trust, governance visibility and controlled decision infrastructure.

---

## Responsible AI Principles

AICOS is designed around human-centric and governance-aware AI system principles.
