# AICOS Architecture Overview

## Vision

AICOS OS is a governance-first decision intelligence infrastructure designed for high-impact environments involving finance, energy, logistics and geopolitical systems.

---

# Core Principles

- Governance-First AI
- Human-Final Authority
- Replayability
- Auditability
- Risk-Aware Systems
- Deterministic Reviewability
- No Autonomous Irreversible Execution

---

# Objective

AICOS is designed to improve operational trust, governance visibility and controlled decision infrastructure for critical environments.
