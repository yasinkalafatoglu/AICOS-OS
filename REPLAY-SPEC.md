# AICOS Replay Specification

## Purpose

AICOS replay infrastructure is designed to help organizations review, replay and audit critical decision flows.

---

## Why Replay Matters

Modern AI systems often generate outputs that cannot easily be reviewed after execution.

AICOS introduces replayable decision infrastructure for high-impact environments.

---

## Replay Flow

```text
Input Data
     ↓
Decision Engine
     ↓
Governance Layer
     ↓
Human Approval
     ↓
Replay & Audit Record
```

---

## Core Principles

- Replayability
- Auditability
- Human-Final Authority
- Governance-First Logic
- Deterministic Reviewability

---

## Objective

The objective of replay systems is not autonomous execution.

The objective is operational trust and decision transparency.
