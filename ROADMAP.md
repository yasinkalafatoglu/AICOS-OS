# AICOS OS Roadmap

## Vision

AICOS OS aims to support governance-first, replayable and human-governed decision infrastructure for high-impact environments.

---

# Phase 1 — Foundation

- Governance principles
- Replay infrastructure concepts
- Architecture documentation
- Security principles
- Human-final authority framework

Status: Active

---

# Phase 2 — Infrastructure Expansion

- Decision replay systems
- Audit infrastructure
- Risk-aware simulation systems
- Governance visibility layers
- Operational trust tooling

Status: In Progress

---

# Phase 3 — Enterprise Integration

- Finance infrastructure support
- Energy systems integration
- Logistics intelligence systems
- Geopolitical monitoring infrastructure

Status: Planned

---

# Phase 4 — Advanced Governance Infrastructure

- Deterministic review systems
- Large-scale replay infrastructure
- Governance-oriented AI orchestration
- Cross-domain operational intelligence

Status: Research

---

# Long-Term Objective

The long-term objective of AICOS OS is to support governable intelligence infrastructure for critical systems operating under uncertainty and risk.
