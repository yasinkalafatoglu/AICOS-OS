# AICOS Governance Principles

## Human-Final Authority

AICOS never performs autonomous irreversible execution.

Human approval remains mandatory for critical decisions.

---

## Replayability

Critical decision flows must remain replayable and reviewable.

---

## Auditability

Decision processes should remain auditable across high-impact environments.

---

## Governance-First AI

Governance is treated as a core system layer, not an optional feature.

---

## Fail-Closed Logic

When uncertainty exceeds acceptable thresholds, systems should halt rather than execute irreversible actions.

---

## Risk-Aware Decision Infrastructure

AICOS is designed for environments involving operational, financial and geopolitical uncertainty.
