# Contributing to AICOS OS

Thank you for your interest in AICOS OS.

AICOS is a governance-first decision intelligence infrastructure focused on replayable, auditable and human-governed AI systems.

---

# Contribution Principles

Contributors should support the following principles:

- Human-Final Authority
- Governance-First AI
- Replayability
- Auditability
- Responsible AI Systems
- Operational Trust
- No Autonomous Irreversible Execution

---

# Contribution Areas

Examples of contribution areas include:

- Governance documentation
- Architecture design
- Replay systems
- Audit infrastructure
- Risk-aware AI systems
- Simulation systems
- Infrastructure research

---

# Collaboration Philosophy

AICOS encourages transparent, responsible and governance-aware technology development.

---

# Important Note

AICOS is designed as a human-centric and governance-oriented infrastructure initiative.
