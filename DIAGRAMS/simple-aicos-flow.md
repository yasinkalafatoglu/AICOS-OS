# Simple AICOS Flow

```text
+-------------------+
|   AI Systems      |
+-------------------+
          |
          v
+-------------------+
| AICOS Governance  |
|       Layer       |
+-------------------+
          |
          v
+-------------------+
| Human Approval    |
+-------------------+
          |
          v
+-------------------+
| Replay & Audit    |
+-------------------+
```

---

## Explanation

Modern AI systems can generate decisions.

AICOS adds a governance layer that helps organizations:

- Keep humans in control
- Replay decisions
- Audit critical events
- Reduce governance risk
- Improve operational trust
